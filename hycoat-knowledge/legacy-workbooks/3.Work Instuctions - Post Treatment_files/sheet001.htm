  Coating Process
                             
  1 Switch on the air drier. Drain the drier filters. Periodically drain the filters.      
  2 Switch on the control Panel Mains.  
  3 Switch on the Booth Blowers. Clean the Booth using dry compressed air.  
  4 Clean the Cyclone, Powder Collector of the Cyclone. ( For every color changes clean the   powder collector, Booth blower chamber etc ).      
  5 Clean the baking oven inside.  
  6 Clean the burner sensor as it should be free form powder and dust particles.  
  7 Check the online gas flow meter and gas pressure 3.5kg/cm2 minimum   
  8 ON the online gas valve  
  9 Switch ON the blower.  
  10 Switch ON the burner  
  11 Set the baking temperature 220 to 230 Deg.c  
  12 Check the temperature indicator, after reaching the set temperature start the process.  
  13 Clean the powder Hose Pipe, Hopper, Injectors, Gun  Nozzle using compressed air.  
  14 After reaching oven temperature to 220 to 230 deg.c, switch on the gun, Control System  
  15 Fill the Hopper with Fresh powder 70% and Sieve Recycle powder 30%.(Mixing  the powder to every  batch  after oven curing check the shade ok,spouse not ok  inform to shift supervisor. )   
  16 Powder coating Gun K V setting 50-100.  
  17 Set the Powder  Fluidisation 0.5 to 1.0.  
  18 Air Pressure  5-7.5 kg/cm².  
  19 Powder  output -0.5 to 3.0 gm/min(4Auto guns)  
  20 Switch ON the conveyor and set the Conveyor indicator speed as per the profile thicknes and speed table.  
  21 Start the Coating process (Auto& Manual Gun distance between component to gun 4 to 10 inches gap)   
  22 After completing coating process switch off the Gun & Control system.  
  23 Switch off the booth blower, Illumination Light.  
  24 Collect the Hopper powder from pack and put in the cotton box  with identification tag.   
  25 Take the collector recovered  powder from  pack and put in separate cotton box with identification  keep Separately.  
                               
  SPECIAL  INSTRUCTIONS: 
  1.Always Stack the Aluminum profiles on the rubber material to avoid scatches and damages.
  2.While handling the components wear Hand gloves, Eye Glasses & Shoes.
  3.Wear mask while doing Coating process.
  4.Always pack fresh powder and recovered powder separately. 
  5. Record the Process Parameters in Setup Approval & IPIR Report as per the Frequency

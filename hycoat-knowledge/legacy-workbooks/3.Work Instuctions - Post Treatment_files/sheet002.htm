Switch on the air drier. Drain the drier filters. Periodically drain the filters.    
Switch on the control Panel Mains.
Switch on the Booth Blower, Illumination light. Clean the Booth using dry compressed air
Clean the Cyclone, Powder Collector of the Cyclone .( For every color changes clean the   powder collector, Booth blower chamber etc ).    
Switch on the Baking Oven as per work instruction ACPL/WI/PC/05 
Clean the powder Hose Pipe, Hopper, Injectors, Gun  Nozzle using compressed air.
After reaching oven temperature to 220 to 230 deg.c, switch on the gun, Control System
Fill the Hopper with Fresh powder 70% and Sieve Recycle powder 30%.(Mixing  the powder to every  batch  after oven curing check the shade ok,spouse not ok  inform to shift supervisor. ) 
Powder coating Gun K V setting 50-100.
Set the Powder  Fluidisation 0.5 to 1.0.
Air Pressure  5-7.5 kg/cm².
Powder  output -0.5 to 3.0 gm/min(4Auto guns)
Switch ON the conveyor and set the Conveyor indicator speed at 41Hz. (conveyor  move at 0.60 m/min).
Start the Coating process (Auto& Manual Gun distance between component to gun 4 to 10 inches gap) 
After completing coating process switch off the Gun & Control system.
Switch off the booth blower, Illumination Light.
Collect the Hopper powder from pack and put in the cotton box  with identification tag. 
Take the collector recovered  powder from  pack and put in separate cotton box with identification  keep Separately.
Operators found following the instruction during Jig cleaning and Coating operator are ensureing proper jig cleaning is done
Special Instructions:
1. Always pack fresh powder and recovered powder separately. 
2. Wear mask while doing Coating process.
3. Record the Process Parameters in Setup Approval & IPIR Report as per the Frequency

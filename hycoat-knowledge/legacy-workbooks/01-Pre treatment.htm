AUTO PRE-TREATMENT

 

 

DEGREASING, DEOXIDIZER & CHROMATION:

 

1.  Check the bath chemical  titration report (ACPL/IPIR/01).

 

2.  Check the Titration pointage twice in a day and if it below the pointage add the chemical

     as per Lab analysis.

                                                                                                                 

3. Check the chemical & rinse water level.

 

4. Load the components in loading trolly jigs. After loading push trolly to  loading  station of auto pretreatment line and  on the switch.                                                                                             Please refer “Pre-Treatment Loading Matrix” for No. of components to be loaded.

 

5.  Automatically transporter  moves to  degreasing tank and dips in  Degreasing tank,   Auto time set at 10 minutes. After 10 minutes lift the components upward direction and wait 10 sec for   dropping remaining chemical  and move to next stage. 

   

6.  Then moves to water rinse 1  Dipping the components in the bath (PH should be 5to 7 if PH value more than 8 then change the water before treatment.) and dip for 1  min. After 1 min lift components  wait for 10 sec dropping  water and move to next stage.(Check the PH twice in a shift.) eck the PH is twice in a shift111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111111

 

7.  Then moves to  water rinse 2  Dip the components in the bath (PH should be 5 to 7, if PH value more than 7 then change the water before the treatment.) and dip for 1 min. After 1 min lift components  wait for 10 sec dropping  water and move to next stage.(Check the PH twice in a shift.)

 

8. Then moves to De-oxidiser  tank and dip the components for 1  min and lift the components upward direction and wait for 10 sec dropping chemical and move to next stage. (Check the bath chemical Titration once in a shift Mixing ratio=1:6)

 

  9.  Then Moves to water rinse -3  (PH should be 5 to 7 if PH value more than 7 then change the water before the treatment.) and dip for 1 min and move to  Chromation tank. (Check the ph twice in a shift.)

 

10.  Then moves to Chromation tank  Dip the components  auto time at  5 min. (Mixing Ratio=5%) After lift the components and wait for 10 sec  dropping remaining chemical and move to next stage. (Do the Titration of bath chemical twice in a shift.)

 

11.  Then moves to water rinse – 4 (PH should be 5 to 7 if  PH value more than 7 then change the water before treatment.)  and dip for 1min then lift the components and wait for 10 sec and after 10 secs lift the components and then moves to next stage.

 

12.  Then moves to Drying oven and keep the components in 15 min. (80°C  to 100°c )After 15 min lift the components and moves to unload station. And   Drop the components jig into unloading station in  empty  trolley.

13.   If  any  problem   Inform  Shift  In charge.

Golden Rules
     
 
Wear safety helmets while in the shop floor
 
Wear safety shoes while in the shop floor
 
Use gloves while handling aluminum Profiles
 
Use safety goggles while working in the coating booths.
 
Aluminum profiles should always be placed/stacked with rubber spacer  to avoid scratches / Damages.
 
Trolleys should be locked when they are loaded with Materials
 
Materials should be tied properly when they are loaded on the trolley for movement.
 
Spacers should be placed between two profiles when loaded on the trolley to avoid scratches.
 
Ensure safety of yourself before helping others

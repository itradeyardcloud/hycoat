    1 Establish and define Standards and Procedure of development requirements for the
product;		 1 Rejection of product(s)/raw material(s)  
    2 Formulate, implement and review work procedures to ensure effectiveness and
efficiency to meet quality requirements  		 2 Appraisal of workers  
    3 Review the verification & validation of product evaluation according to ISO requirements and actively provide constructive feedback to production/development team 3 Sanction leaves to workers  
    4 Participate in risk and hazard assessment during design & development of the product.        
    5 Establish and maintain high quality test procedures and methods to be used in present and future product testing and development that comply with the company quality standards and deliverables.        
    6 Oversee the resolution of quality-related problems in the development and manufacturing environment resulting from supply or production imperfections.        
    7 Ensure the proper maintenance of document control processes and devices.       
    8 Manage and maintain the Company’s quality inspection and product release programs for incoming and in-process materials and components, processes and finished goods.      
    9 Establish an auditing program and lead compliance audits of third party suppliers, manufacturers and distributors.      
    10 Report on timely basis to executive management on the performance of the quality system, any non-compliance issues and recommended actions.
